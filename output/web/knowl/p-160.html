Web-based forums have some similarities to email lists because users can subscribe to receive updates via email. However, conversations in forums are typically grouped by discussion topic and are often better-organized than email archives, which makes them easier to browse and search. People on a web forum typically have handles or nicknames, rarely using their full real name, unlike in email. It's FOSS Community 30  is an example of an active forum on FOSS.
