In open source, documentation is a key tool of transparency and participation. You have to document everything that you do in a open source project, not just the code. Next year when you need to remember why a technical decision was made, the answer is coming from a combination of mailing list archives, wiki page history, and well written version control log messages. A question of why you have specific commit rules or code styles? Mailing list archive and wiki page history are your friends.
