Item. Wikis.

Although Wikis can be created from many of the SECPs, they are discussed apart from these because the concept of a wiki is so widely utilized and understood due to the wide popularity of Wikipedia 6  and WikiMedia 7  . Simply stated, a wiki is a web site where the pages are a user-editable, easy-to-use, asynchronous way of putting semi-permanent content on the web, so they are useful for documentation, timelines, status reports, meeting minutes, and conversation logs. For the writer, a wiki provides easy version control, a database backend, simplified markup, and a fast edit-and-post cycle.

Authoring and maintaining pages in a wiki is a critical part of some wiki-driven OSS projects. If you’re particularly interested in a project, register an account and start contributing.
